French DELF B2 Exam

DELF B2 French Exam

DELF B2 French Exam is administered by the CIEP on behalf of the French Ministry of Education. At the B2 level of proficiency - which corresponds to an upper intermediate level in French - the user can manage quite comfortably in French language in most situations.

The B2 level of proficiency is the minimum requirement for:
  • Study in France, Study in Switzerland
  • Quebec immigration

DELF B2 Exam Components

  1. DELF B2 Listening
  2. DELF B2 Written Comprehension (Reading)
  3. DELF B2 Oral Production (Speaking)
  4. DELF B2 Written Production (Writing)
