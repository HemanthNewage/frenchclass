French DALF B1 Exam

DELF B1 French Exam

At the DELF B1 level, the language user becomes independent. The student can interact more; he / she can understand and maintain a discussion and give his / her opinion. The learner is also capable of dealing with various situations likely to arise in daily life.

DELF B1 Exam Components

  1. DELF B1 Oral Comprehension (Listening)
  2. DELF B1 Comprehension Ecrite (Reading)
  3. DELF B1 Oral Production (Speaking)
  4. DELF B1 Written Production (Writing)
