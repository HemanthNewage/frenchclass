French DALF A2 Exam

DELF A2 French Exam

DELF A2 recognizes the linguistic competency of a basic user. At the A2 level, the learner can communicate in simple and routine tasks requiring the most common polite phrases and exchanges of information.

DELF A2 Exam Components

  1. DELF A2 Oral Comprehension (Listening)
  2. DELF A2 Comprehension Ecrite (Reading)
  3. DELF A2 Oral Production (Speaking)
  4. DELF A2 Written Production (Writing)

DELF A2 Exam Format

Nature of the exams: A2 Duration

The overall pass mark is 50/100. The pass mark per test is 5/25.
